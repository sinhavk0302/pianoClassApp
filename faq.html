Frequently Asked Questions

What age is best to start piano lessons?

While children can start as young as 4 or 5, the "best" age varies. We recommend starting when a child shows interest and can focus for about 30 minutes. We teach students of all ages, including adults!

Do I need my own piano or keyboard?

Yes, having an instrument at home for regular practice is essential for progress. It doesn't need to be an expensive acoustic piano initially; a good quality digital keyboard with 88 weighted keys is often a great starting point.

How often should I practice?

Consistency is key! For beginners, we recommend aiming for 15-30 minutes, 4-5 days a week. More advanced students will typically need longer practice sessions. We'll discuss personalized practice plans during lessons.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for lesson cancellations. Lessons cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged. Please see our Pricing page for full details.

How do I schedule and pay for lessons?

You can view available times and book lessons through our online Schedule page. Payments for packages or individual lessons can be made securely online via Stripe or PayPal (Note: requires backend integration).
