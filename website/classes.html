Our Piano Lessons

We offer a variety of lesson formats to suit different ages, skill levels, and musical interests. All lessons are tailored to the individual student.

Beginner Piano Lessons

Perfect for those with little or no prior experience. Learn the fundamentals of piano playing, including note reading, rhythm, basic chords, and proper technique in a fun and encouraging environment.

Duration: Typically 30 or 45 minutes.

Focus: Building foundational skills, simple songs, music theory basics.

Intermediate Piano Lessons

For students who have mastered the basics and are ready to tackle more complex pieces and techniques. Develop musicality, explore different genres, and refine your skills.

Duration: Typically 45 or 60 minutes.

Focus: More challenging repertoire, scales and arpeggios, dynamics, expression, improvisation basics.

Advanced Piano Lessons

Designed for experienced players looking to master advanced repertoire, prepare for exams or auditions, or delve deeply into specific musical styles.

Duration: Typically 60 minutes.

Focus: Complex classical or contemporary pieces, advanced theory and harmony, performance techniques, interpretation.

Genre-Specific Lessons (e.g., Jazz, Pop)

Interested in a particular style? We offer specialized lessons focusing on the unique techniques, theory, and repertoire of genres like Jazz, Pop, Blues, etc.
